Our guide for codespaces

Why use codespaces?

Codespaces are useful as they allow you to generete your own links that are unblocked.

How to make one

Codespaces are simple. First, make a github account or log in. Next, go to the code you want a codespace for. You should see a green button on the top right. Click it, then click "codespaces", then click "create a codespace on main" or master.

Once it loads, go to the terminal and type these in this order: npm i, npm start.
